About

The Jake Project is an American pop/rock band, with a distinctive retro-inspired, yet fresh sound, deeply rooted in blues, Southern rock and jazz.

Led by singer Jake Sears and an evolving, all-star cast of collaborating musicians, the band’s stylistic leanings evoke shades of music’s greatest past and present figures, from Dave Matthews Band to Phish, Buffalo Springfield to Alabama Shakes.
